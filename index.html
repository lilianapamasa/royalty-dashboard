Recommendations
Immediate
Audit November + 6 negative payments
Three partners collapsed simultaneously in November — this is systemic, not a partner issue. Investigate the batch job or data feed. All 6 negative processed payments require review and likely reversal.
Short-term
Enforce stream count as a required field
19 records (10%) were paid with no stream count — no audit trail, no way to validate the rate. Payments with null stream count should be held pending and routed for manual review before processing.
Long-term
Build automated rate-per-stream alerting
Flag any payment where the rate deviates >2σ from the partner's 6-month rolling baseline before payout. This catches both sudden spikes like Brightwave UK and slow drift like Harborlight AU automatically.
