Recommendations
Immediate
Audit November + 6 negative payments
Three partners collapsed simultaneously in November — this is a systemic event, not a partner issue. Investigate the batch job or data feed. All 6 negative processed payments require individual review and likely reversal.
Short-term
Enforce stream count as required field
19 records (10%) were paid with no stream count — no audit trail, no way to validate the rate. Payments with a null stream count should be held as pending and routed for manual review before processing.
Long-term
Build rate-per-stream alerting
Implement a monitor that flags any payment where the rate deviates >2σ from the partner's 6-month rolling baseline before payout. This would have surfaced both the Brightwave UK spike and the Harborlight AU drift automatically.
